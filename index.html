Our Story

Discover the passion behind Cozy & Chic Finds

Who We Are

Cozy & Chic Finds was born from a simple idea: that everyone deserves to live in a beautiful, comfortable space and wear clothes that make them feel confident, without breaking the bank.

Founded in 2018 by interior designer Emma Richardson and fashion stylist James Peterson, our team has grown to include passionate curators who scour Amazon to find the very best products that combine quality, style, and value.

We're not just another affiliate site - we're a team of real people who test, review, and stand behind every product we recommend.

Our Process

Every product we feature goes through a rigorous selection process. We look for items that meet our high standards for quality, design, and value.

1

Initial Screening

We analyze thousands of products based on reviews, materials, and brand reputation.

2

Hands-On Testing

Our team personally tests products in real-life scenarios to evaluate quality and functionality.

3

Final Approval

Only products that pass all our tests make it to our curated collections.
